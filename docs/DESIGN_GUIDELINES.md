# Design Guidelines

## Brand Direction

The app's visual language should follow the main logo and the paper blue-gray palette:

- App background: paper blue-gray (`#f6f8fa`)
- Feature surface: soft blue-gray (`#edf4f8`)
- Primary text: deep slate (`#1f2f3d`)
- Primary action/progress: ink blue (`#24445c`)
- Secondary text: muted blue-gray (`#60717f`)
- Soft border: pale blue-gray (`#d6e2ea`)
